Rubik’s Cube

Did You Know?

  • Erno Rubik, inventor of the Cube, originally didn’t want the puzzle to be solvable
  • The Cube was reinvented in 2003, including faster action mechanisms and tougher tiles
  • The largest working Rubik’s Cube is 17x17x17 constructed by Oskar van Deventer of the Netherlands
