When to Stop Trick-or-Treating

Provided that you are wearing an awesome costume that is entertaining and unique, you should be able to Trick-or-Treat forever. I know a lot of people think it is pathetic for anyone older than a 13-year-old to go out trick-or-treating, but I totally disagree. Trick-or-treating is just plain old fun, and, not to mention, a great outlet for one's creativity!
